History

From a weekend pop-up to a neighborhood favorite

Harbor & Hearth began as a small weekend cart serving pour-over coffee at local community events. As our customer family grew, so did the dream of creating a permanent space that felt warm, creative, and grounded.

Today, the shop continues that same spirit by supporting local bakers, hosting small gatherings, and serving every guest like a regular.
